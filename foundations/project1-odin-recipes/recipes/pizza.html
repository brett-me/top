Description

This lasagna recipe is courtesy of the Foodbank in Australia.
Serves 6 and takes 1 hour and 15 minutes to prepare and cook.

Ingredients

  • 150ml warm water
  • 1 tsp of sugar
  • 15g fresh yeast
  • 225g of plain flour
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 80g of tomato passata
  • 70g mozzarella
  • Pinch of oregano
  • 1 Basil leaf
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Pre heat oven to 180°C.
  2. Wash and dry vegetables thoroughly. To do this rinse vegetables well with cool water, being sure to remove any dirt or residue. Pat dry using a clean paper towel or cloth.
  3. Peel the onion and dice finely.
  4. Heat oil in a saucepan and add the onion, cook until browned.
  5. Add mince to the saucepan, stirring occasionally. Cook until browned, then add herbs.
  6. Add crushed tomato and tomato paste to the saucepan and stir to combine. Simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Grease a large baking dish and line the base with a layer of lasagna sheets.
  8. Pour one third of the mince mixture on top of lasagne sheets and sprinkle approximately ½ a cup of grated cheese on top.
  9. Continue to layer the lasagne sheets, mince mixture and grated cheese until mince is used up.
  10. Finish with a layer of lasagne sheets on top and sprinkle with cheese.
  11. Cover with aluminium foil and bake for 45 minutes.
  12. Remove foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes to brown the top.
