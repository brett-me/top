Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is courtesy of the Foodbank in Australia.
Serves 6 and takes 1 hour and 15 minutes to prepare and cook.

Ingredients

  • 1 zucchini (grated)
  • 2x 400g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • ½ teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 250g lasagne sheets
  • 2 cups grated cheese
  • 2 teaspoons canola oil
  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 onion (diced)
  • 1 carrot (finely chopped)
  • 100g can tomato paste

Steps

  1. Pre heat oven to 180°C.
  2. Wash and dry vegetables thoroughly. To do this rinse
    vegetables well with cool water, being sure to remove
    any dirt or residue. Pat dry using a clean paper towel or cloth.
  3. Peel the onion and dice finely.
  4. Heat oil in a saucepan and add the onion, cook until browned.
  5. Add mince to the saucepan, stirring occasionally. Cook until
    browned, then add herbs.
  6. Add crushed tomato and tomato paste to the saucepan and stir
    to combine. Simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Grease a large baking dish and line the base with a layer of
    lasagna sheets.
  8. Pour one third of the mince mixture on top of lasagne sheets
    and sprinkle approximately ½ a cup of grated cheese on top.
  9. Continue to layer the lasagne sheets, mince mixture and
    grated cheese until mince is used up.
  10. Finish with a layer of lasagne sheets on top and sprinkle
    with cheese.
  11. Cover with aluminium foil and bake for 45 minutes.
  12. Remove foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes to brown
    the top.
